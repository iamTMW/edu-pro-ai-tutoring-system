Module 2: Subtraction

Subtraction means taking away or finding how many are left.

What Subtraction Means

If you have something and you use some, lose some, or give some away, subtraction helps us find how many remain.

We show subtraction with the – sign.

Example idea: If I have 10 cookies and I eat 3, subtraction helps me find how many cookies are left, which are 7 cookies left.

How to Think About Subtraction

We can subtract by:

  • Counting backwards
  • Using a number line
  • Taking away physical objects
  • Thinking in terms of “How many left?”

Tens and Ones in Subtraction

Sometimes we need to “borrow” when subtracting.

We focus on:

  • Taking away ones from ones
  • Taking away tens from tens

Why Subtraction Is Important

We use subtraction to:

  • Count how much is left
  • Compare two amounts
  • Figure out differences
  • Solve everyday problems
